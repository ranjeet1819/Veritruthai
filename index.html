Core Forensic Backbone Technologies

🖼️ Media Forensics Stack

  • MTCNN Face Alignment: Automatically locates, clips, and normalizes specific facial structures across frames to isolate evaluation fields.
  • EfficientNet-V2 Classifier: Analyzes blended boundary edges and localized fake pixel distortions with 96.4% verified accuracy.
  • rPPG Heartbeat Monitor: Tracks sub-visual dermal skin color periodicities to prove biological life and flag non-human blood flow waves.

📄 NLP Synthetic Text Stack

  • RoBERTa-Base Model: Trained over massive synthetic corpuses to detect structural tokens matching OpenAI or LLM generation footprints.
  • Perplexity Metric Analysis: Computes the mathematical probability weight of word choices to find out if the text flow is too predictable.
  • Burstiness Variance Mapping: Measures sentence length consistency, automatically flagging flat-line non-human sentence layouts.
